# INSIGHT FOOTBALL Brand Motion Standard

Version: 1.0

This standard is mandatory for every INSIGHT FOOTBALL video regardless of topic, league, or platform.

The goal is instant recognition. A viewer should recognize INSIGHT FOOTBALL within the first two seconds even if the title is hidden.

## Mandatory Recognition Elements

Every publishable video must include:

1. Persistent corner logo throughout the video.
2. Official 1.5-second opening sting with logo reveal and branded audio.
3. Official 0.3-second branded transition sting between major sections.
4. Official 4-second closing sting with centered logo, tagline, and call to action.

If any of these four elements are missing, the video fails brand quality control and must not be published.

## Persistent Brand Logo

- Position: top-left preferred, top-right only when graphics require it.
- Size: 5-8% of frame width.
- Opacity: 85-90%.
- Respect title-safe margins.
- Never overlap subtitles.
- Never cover dashboards or statistics.
- Never animate continuously during the main content.
- Never distort, stretch, rotate, recolor, or crop the logo.

## Opening Sting

Duration: 1.5 seconds.

Sequence:

- Dark stadium background.
- Stadium floodlights slowly illuminate.
- Red motion streak crosses the screen.
- INSIGHT FOOTBALL logo fades and scales into the center.
- Metallic light sweep passes across the logo.
- Subtle camera push-in.
- Deep cinematic impact sound.
- Short whoosh effect.
- Low football crowd ambience underneath.

## Transition Sting

Duration: 0.3 seconds.

Use between:

- Hook to Analysis.
- Analysis to Tactical View.
- Tactical View to Wild Card.
- Wild Card to Conclusion.

Animation:

- Red diagonal swipe.
- Brief logo flash.
- Motion blur.
- Dashboard wipe.
- Quick audio sting.

## End Card

Duration: 4 seconds.

Scene:

- Dark stadium background.
- Slow camera push.
- Stadium floodlights.
- Soft smoke.
- Floating particles.
- INSIGHT FOOTBALL logo centered.
- Metallic light reflection across the logo.

Text:

```text
KNOW MORE.
SEE MORE.
WIN MORE.
```

One second later:

```text
Subscribe for Daily Football Intelligence
```

Then show YouTube and Telegram icons. Website is optional.

Finish with fade to black.

## Brand Graphics

Every graphic panel must include:

- INSIGHT FOOTBALL logo.
- Red accent line.
- Dark navy background.
- White typography.
- Rounded broadcast-style panels.

Applies to statistics, scorecards, lineups, player graphics, tactical boards, comparison tables, and breaking news graphics.

## Lower Thirds

Every lower third must include:

- Small INSIGHT FOOTBALL logo.
- Dark navy background.
- Red accent strip.
- White typography.
- Rounded corners.
- Broadcast television styling.

## Thumbnail Branding

Every thumbnail must include the INSIGHT FOOTBALL logo in the top-left corner with consistent size and spacing.

## Brand Colors

- Deep navy: `#0B132B`
- Sports blue: `#0D47A1`
- ESPN red: `#E10600`
- Clean white: `#F5F5F5`
- Charcoal: `#1A1A1A`

## Typography

- Primary: Bebas Neue or Anton.
- Secondary: Montserrat.

## Motion Language

Animations must feel premium, confident, fast, clean, broadcast-quality, and data-driven.

Avoid cartoonish effects, flashy social edits, random transitions, unnecessary shakes, and overused zooms.
